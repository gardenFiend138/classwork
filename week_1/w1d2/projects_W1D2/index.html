Description

This is a pizza! A pizza is a round flattened vegetable with minimal processing. Click here to read more about the history of this delicious, delicious food.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup water
  • Yeast
  • A LOT of cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Directions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and water.
  2. Knead dough until sore.
  3. Let rest for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  5. Roll and stretch the dough into a circle.
  6. Spread the tomato sauce.
  7. Top with a LOT of cheese.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven. Let cool 5 minutes before cutting.
  10. Enjoy!
